Admissions

We welcome students of all backgrounds. Here's how to apply:

Admission Process

  • Visit the school office for the application form.
  • Submit necessary documents (birth certificate, previous transcripts).
  • Attend an entrance test and interview (if applicable).
  • Receive confirmation of admission and pay fees.

Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate and recent photos
  • Past academic records

Important Dates

Admissions typically begin in Baisakh (April). Visit or contact us for current deadlines.
